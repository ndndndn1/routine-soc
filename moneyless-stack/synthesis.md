Cross-combo synthesis. Updated only by task (d).

## State after run 1

Only combo-A (L1 × L3) has been surveyed. The early signal suggests two
engineering primitives are recurring and likely load-bearing for the
moneyless stack:

1. **PINN-driven health monitoring** as an L1 sensor-to-indicator
   reduction. It lets a node produce a calibrated health estimate from
   physical first principles without needing a labelled failure corpus.
2. **Peer-ranked / reputation-weighted consensus** as an L3 primitive
   that does not require a token economy. Useful because it lets
   "commit to ledger" cost scale with disagreement, not with compute.

Too early to call a direction. Re-evaluate after combo-B and combo-C.
